Welcome!

The SHPS Shop Platform System is a robust and user-friendly application designed to streamline retail operations for businesses of all sizes.

This comprehensive point-of-sale (POS) solution offers features such as inventory management, sales tracking, and customer relationship management.

With its intuitive interface, SHPS enables staff to process transactions quickly and efficiently, enhancing the overall shopping experience for customers.

The system supports various payment methods and integrates seamlessly with e-commerce platforms, making it ideal for both in-store and online sales.

Additionally, SHPS provides detailed reporting and analytics, empowering retailers to make informed decisions and optimize their operations.

Note: If installed in a folder on drive C (Windows), always run as an administrator to avoid closure on function calls.

This program is written in Golang.
